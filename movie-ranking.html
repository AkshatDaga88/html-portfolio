My top 3 movies of all time

Shawshank Redemption

The movie showcases the changes prison makes in a man and the quest of an innocent person to escape prison while helping his fellow prisonmates upskill and better themselves.


Andaz Apna Apna

It is a story about two happy-go-lucky guys trying to woo a rich heiress with comedy ensuing as they continue their quest.


Pokemon: The Legend of Entei

Ash returns to save his mother from being trapped by a girl who is possessed by Unowns and has released Entei from its sleep.
